Academic bio

I earned my Master’s degree in the Cognitive & Information Sciences program at UC Merced, where I contributed to research in Dr. Zenaida Aguirre-Muñoz’s Language,Learning, and Teaching Lab and Dr. Rachel Ryskin’s Language, Interaction, & Cognition (LInC) lab. Currently, I serve as the lab manager in Dr. Zenaida’s lab, continuing my collaboration with both professors. My research focuses on bilingual sentence processing, particularly on how second language learners process sentences.

I completed my undergraduate studies in Special Education (B.Ed.) at National Tsing Hua University. Moreover, I have a keen interest in how technology can facilitate language learning, with a particular focus on reading education.
