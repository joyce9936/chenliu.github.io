Academic bio

I earned my Master’s degree in the Cognitive & Information Sciences program at UC Merced, where I contributed to research in Dr. Zenaida Aguirre-Muñoz’s Language,Learning, and Teaching Lab and Dr. Rachel Ryskin’s Language, Interaction, & Cognition (LInC) lab. Currently, I serve as the lab manager in Dr. Zenaida’s lab, continuing my collaboration with both professors.

My research interest falls on how digital technologies can facilitate language learning, with a particular emphasis on reading education. I’m interested in exploring how innovative tools such as eBooks, Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR), and AI-Powered Reading Assistants can be effectively designed and integrated into classroom settings to enhance language acquisition and reading comprehension, especially in bilingual contexts.
